Open Source and Open Governance

The Hawaii Project is a meritocratic, consensus-based community interested in developing the Hawaii system. We believe in the power of Open Source, Free Software and worldwide collaboration as we want to build, share and improve software together and make it available to everyone.

Out of the Box Experience

Hawaii aims to offer an excellent user experience with its desktop environment. Instead of forcing you to know every detail of your computer we expect you to turn it on, sit down and just use it.

Advanced Technologies

Hawaii was born to take advantage of QtQuick, Wayland and systemd. Therefore it features a dynamic, flicker free and fast system.

Responsive Design

Hawaii was made to look and behave great on every form factor. The user interface is adapted to the needs of the device it is running on, this means that while on desktops you have more elements and a layout optimized for mouse and keyboard interaction, on mobile you can use applications that adapts to the screen resolution and are designed for touch.
